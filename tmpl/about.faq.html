Q: Why did you make this site?

A: I was bored one day, and I wanted my own space on the massive superhighway of the internet, so why not :P.


Q: Why does it look all outdated? Why not use modern design?

A: Personally, I Hate modern web design with a capital H.

Many modern websites look like they were thrown together in Microsoft Paint in 30 seconds, and then turned the resource usage and thrasing up to 11.

Oh, and don't forget the absurd amount of pop-ups, ads, tracking, and data-mining/selling!

The old internet was when sites had personality, and the internet wasn't some coporate breeding ground. I much prefer this era... despite me being born near the end of it.

So basically, I'm trying to relive the old days, when you could say, do, and find anything and have fun going explorihe internet, and without any ads, tracking, or similar.

Unlike nowadays where most people are relegated to 3 - 6 sites, most of those harvest your data and sell them for prng on tofit (Looking at you, Facebook/Meta and Google.), as well as bombard you with ads and other pop-ups.
