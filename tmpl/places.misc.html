These are just some miscellaneous cool sites. Have a look:

WinWorld

An abandonware enthusaist's dream. Filled with tons and tons of old/obscure software.
Internet Archive

A massive online library of websites, software, books, audio, video, and more. A data hoarder's wet dream.
wiby.me

A search engine for old Web 1.0 sites. I like to go on ancient website expeditions using this site.
NINA

NINA is a revival service for Yahoo! Messenger, with America Online, AOL Instant Messenger, Old-school ICQ, and Quantum-Link coming soon.
Escargot

Escargot is a revival service for MSN/Windows Live Messenger and related services that was launched in 2017. NINA's sister project.
Fun-lover.com

Fun-lover.com is a website that includes a lot of cool things. Mainly related to 90s/early 2000s technology. It includes a lot of stuff relating to WebTV/MSN TV, MIDI and MOD music, and some tools.
