These are the rules for the Garry's Mod server. Generally, it just boils down to common sense:


  • Don't use hacks, exploits, cheats, etc to gain an unfair advantage or otherwise make the game unfun/unfair for other players.
  • Don't purposefully slow down the server (by DDOS, for example)
  • No bigotry whatsoever, no matter what. This includes racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, etc.
  • No NSFW content. Risque memes are fine, downright porn isn't.
  • This server isn't your personal playground (it's everyone's playground, gameplay wise :P), don't bring your own drama and bullshit here.
  • Try to stay civil when discussing something controversial like politics.
  • Please don't backseat moderate. Leave moderation up to actual staff, as you're a mere peasant (/s).

  • Profit!
